Plant Disease Detection

About the Project

The Plant Disease Detection using Image Processing project introduces a sophisticated method for detecting plant diseases at an early stage using advanced image processing techniques. By analyzing images of plants, the system identifies diseases with remarkable accuracy, helping farmers take immediate corrective actions.

Leveraging cutting-edge machine learning models and computer vision, the system categorizes different plant diseases and provides insights on severity and treatment. It revolutionizes the way farmers monitor crop health, significantly reducing the manual effort and time required for diagnosis.

Our goal is to empower agriculture with technology by providing a scalable and efficient solution for early disease detection, improving crop yield and sustainability globally.
